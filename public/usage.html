How to Use the Attendance Calculation App

1. Opening the App:

Open the app either on your computer or mobile device. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

2. Understanding the Interface:

When the app opens, you'll see a form with input fields for males, females, children, and cars. These fields are where you input the attendance data.

3. Entering Attendance Data:

Start by entering the number of males, females, and children who attended your event or gathering in their respective input fields. If there were cars involved (for example, if it's a driving event), enter the number of cars in the designated field.

4. Calculating Total Attendance:

After entering all the attendance data, click the "Calculate Total Attendance" button. This action will calculate and display the total attendance for males, females, children, and cars.

5. Generating a Report:

Once the total attendance is calculated, you can click the "Print Report" button to generate a PDF report summarizing the attendance details. The report includes the number of males, females, children, and cars, making it easy to share and review the attendance information.

6. Sharing the Report (Optional):

To share the PDF report, you can upload it to a cloud storage service like Google Drive or Dropbox and then share the link with others. Alternatively, if you have a WhatsApp account and the necessary permissions, you can use the app to send the PDF report directly to a WhatsApp number.

7. Reviewing the Total Attendance:

After generating the report, you'll also see the total attendance displayed within the app interface. This summary helps you quickly check the overall attendance figures.

8. Closing the App:

Once you've finished using the app and reviewing the attendance data, you can close the app or navigate to other sections as needed.

If you encounter any issues or have questions while using the app, feel free to refer to the app's documentation or seek assistance from the app's support team. Happy attendance tracking!
